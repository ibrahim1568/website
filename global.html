    Class Description

    Global Economics is an intensive college course delving into the intricate dynamics of the world's economic landscape, highlighting the interplay of international economies, the significance of global trade and finance, and the challenges and opportunities arising from globalization. Covering key topics such as international trade, financial markets, trade policies, and economic development, this course aims to foster a deep understanding of global economics, emphasizing the interconnectedness of economies and their global impact. Through case studies and real-world applications, students are equipped to critically analyze and address pressing global economic challenges, making informed decisions in our increasingly interdependent world.
